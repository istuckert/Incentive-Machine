About This Platform

The Manufactured Divide is an independent platform dedicated to mapping verifiable incentive structures across major American policy sectors, including immigration, healthcare, defense, and banking. Every claim is grounded in primary government data, peer-reviewed research, or documented investigative reporting, with direct source links provided for verification. The platform illuminates how large-scale systems interact, how concentrated incentives shape outcomes, and how surface-level debates often obscure underlying mechanisms. Its goal is to equip users with transparent, evidence-based context that supports more informed analysis and decision-making. The project maintains strict political neutrality. While perfect objectivity is unattainable, every assertion is supported by primary sources to allow independent verification.

AI Disclosure

Research on this platform was conducted by a human drawing on primary sources. AI was used to assist with coding, visual organization, and document refinement. Editorial judgment, source selection, and factual verification are entirely human.
